What is Maya?
Maya is your friendly Singapore Malay learning companion! Practice conversational Malay, test your knowledge with quizzes, and engage in real-life scenarios.

Features:
🗣️ Interactive Chat: Practice conversations and role-playing scenarios with English translations
🧠 Knowledge Quiz: Test your vocabulary and grammar across 14 different themes
📅 Daily Phrases: Learn essential phrases through an 8-week structured program
🌍 Singapore Context: Learn authentic Singapore Malay expressions

How to use:
Click on the tabs above to navigate between different learning modes. Start with Daily Phrases to learn essential vocabulary, then practice with Chat conversations!
